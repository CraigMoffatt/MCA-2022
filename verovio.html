Week 3 - Task 2

Difference 1

MEI understands what something is in the music, it groups chords together and makes it much more simplistic to read, MusicXML does not do this however and presents everything as its own individual entity

Difference 2

The coding of the accidental markings are different between MEI and MusicXML - MEI displays accidentals through letters - being f for flat, n for natural and s for sharp - whereas MusicXML presents it through numbers, being 1 for sharps and -1 for flats

Difference 3

MEI assigns everything its own unique code to help identify where it is in the music score, this can also be used to edit the notes – I imagine in the case of a repeat it would say in the code to repeat back to a certain bar and give its unique code – MXML identifies where something is through its location, by giving it an X and Y coordinate style thing, which corresponds to where it is on the document
